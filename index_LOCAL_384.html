Introduction

Founded in 2006, CRA-Electronics. set out itself to be a leading electronic firm in the property, corporate, industrial, commercial, and construction industries. The company is a member of the electronic Association (EIA). We have branches in major towns like Barcelona, Velancia and Alecante. Our Barcelona branch is now open to cater for our customers at the coast region.

Misson Statement

CRA-Electronics mission is to:
  • Provide high-quality, affordable electronic equipments
  • Create and cultivate long-term relationships with clients
  • Respond immediately to the changing needs of our clients
  • Achieve complete customer satisfaction
  • Improve our services continuously
  • Maintain professional relationships with our dedicated workers by encouraging ongoing communication to achieve the highest standard of performance.

Vision

The vision at CRA-Electronics, is of a highly trained and efficient team of workers and customer service personnel, ready for anything that may occur and quick to respond to customer needs and concerns. Mission At CRA-electronics every person in our company is a member of the team and a team player, with the expectation and need that they perform their duties to their fullest capacity and potential. Our objective is a commitment to the highest quality electronic equipments and customer service. The client satisfaction is our main focus, reached through innovative and cost effective security services. We will lead the security industry in trained workers and rapid customer service.

Values

At CRA-Eletronics, there are several values which we hold in high regard, and we expect these values in every member of our team. These values are charity, honesty, fortitude, reliability, integrity, temperance, faith, and hope. We believe that these values set us a step above the rest, and allow our team members to provide high quality security services in a very professional and efficient manner.
